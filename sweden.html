La Santa María Tattoo

We are La Santa Maria Tattoo, a shop dedicated to the body art. Our focus is to give a clean work and in hygienic environment, have a good time while you are there and walk out with a tattoo you will enjoy until the end of time.

Artists

EMMA BJELKE

Emma Bjelke has been tattooing since late 2016 and is one of the full time tattooers at La Santa Maria Tattoo in Örebro. Before tattooing Emma studied fine arts at Örebro Art College for three years and were one of the founders of Bunkern, an art collective who works to promote and exhibit young and upcoming artists. As a tattooer she is working within a broad spectrum from minimalistic blackwork to realism and color, although her favorite style is "some kind of neo traditional.

ALEXANDRA ANDERSEN

Alexandra is originally from Oslo, but also lived a couple of years in Santiago De Chile. She doing piercing in our studio and have been apprentice from Gian Carlo “Chango” Ferrari. Her working and education background is an enrolled nurse, she also went to art school in Oslo.

JORGE HERRERA

Jorge tattooer and owner at La Santa Maria Tattoo. Made his first tattoo when he was 15 years old with homemade tattoo machine, in 2005 he started to tattooing at home learning by himself, originally from Santiago Chile, his work is focus on black and grey , chicano style, lettering and colors, during his process of learning how to tattoo he was studying Industrial Design for 3 years.
